Akhan nu teri surat jach gayi,
Dil nu jach geya tu sajna,
Dass horan nu vekh ke ki karne,
Mainu labh geya tu sajna.

Kinjh kaha main dil diyan gallan,
Dil diyan gallan keh na paava,
Tu tan kalla reh paina aa,
Main tere bin reh na paava.

Lokkan layi tan chann pyara,
Mere layi tera muh sajna,
Akhan nu teri surat jach gayi,
Dil nu jach geya.
